Header

  • Target Audience

    For this composition the target audience of this site would be people in the industrial field. The location of such industries are located in high populated areas or near mining sites, or where resources are more plentiful and mainly companies or people that work with equipment used in the industrial field. The field can consist of people(mainly men), between the ages of 20-50. While the age span is pretty broad, the 20 year olds are mostly the crew while the 50 year olds are mostly the owners or higher ups in the company.

  • Client/User Needs

    The client goals for this site are people with a financial stability, have enough money to purchase such industrial equipment, or know how to operate industrial equipment and to give positive feedback to this company. People in the industrial field are either building or produce something for themselves using this companies products or services, or another company has hired them to build or produce something for them. The people who use the products or services of this company want information about the company to get a better understanding of the products and services they provide. The client would be somebody or a different company wanting something from this company that could provide such equipment for them.

  • Critique

    For this companies header, I notice that the "phone" and "location" text and icons could be a different color due to it is kind of hard to see with the grey background color. The logo text could be in a different font family for those who do not know how to read cursive. The light blue in the text signifies stability, trust, wisdom, loyalty, faith, truth, intelligence, and confidence to the users. The use of the orange in the navigational tags and the search icon helps symbolize a sense of strength, happiness, joy, and determination to the users. The black background with the white navigational text sends the message of power with a touch of purity.

CTA

  • Target Audience

    The target audience for this composition would be anybody between the ages of 18 and up. I say 18 and up because most installation companies tend to make contracts for people to sign at the age of 18 or older. Gender could be both female or male. Location could be a residence or in a work place.

  • Client/User Needs

    For this CTA, the digital installation company has set up awareness of what exactly they do and how users can get more information by switching to digital or raise awareness by letting their users have additional information. The CTA looks professional and the client can use brand recognition from the image used in the CTA, letting their users find out what their product and services are by having a professional looking CTA. The users of digital installation want to have the best services for their viewing experience and this can be done by looking at the layout of the CTA. Users tend to trust a site that looks professional than sites that are dull or that lack specific information or no additional information.

  • Critique

    The use of the red buttons draws attention to people who view this website to attain more information about what this company does or what the benefits of digital viewing compared to different methods of viewing. The buttons used are sized proportionately for easy recognition and the red brings out what the company loves to do or provide for their users. The columns are laid out in a way for easy reading and the text pops out to the viewers due to the font size and font family usage. I like how they used the "W" is "Switch" to indicate two V's, with the first V switching over to the next V which helps the flow of eye movement over the text. The image used sends a clear message that the company is about digital viewing and the use of black and blue blends in together letting the viewers know that they are a formal and trustworthy company. Everything is aligned correctly and the use of the elements in the CTA are attention grabbing to those who view this CTA.
